Soft OLED vs Hard OLED: Which iPhone Screen Grade Should You Buy?

Direct Answer

Soft OLED is usually the better choice for premium repair markets, Hard OLED fits price-sensitive buyers who still want OLED display quality, and Incell LCD is suitable for cost-controlled repairs.

Comparison Table

OptionBest forKey pointBuying advice
Soft OLEDPremium repair shopsFlexible, vivid, closer to original feelBest for higher-value customers
Hard OLEDWholesalers and price-sensitive marketsOLED color at lower costGood for balanced price and quality
Incell LCDFast repair and budget channelsLower price, stable supplyUse for cost-focused volume orders
